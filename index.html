Zheng Xin Yuan

Situated right in the middle of the Senior High block, Zheng Xin Yuan is a place where many Dunmanians would pass by after their morning flag raising ceremony. It is also where the iconic Kong Zi statue is located

Hall

The hall is the place where many of our assemblies are held as well as many concerts for the JH section. Many Dunmanians will remember the Hall as the place where they first got their school badges during their orientation.

Performing Arts Centre

The PAC is where many performances as well as talks and assemblies are held.

Indoor Sports Hall(ISH)

The ISH is a place where many sports CCAs train for their competitions and also where some PE lessons are held
