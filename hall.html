School Hall

The hall is a where our school holds many events such as talks, concerts, etc. Other than for formal events, our school also uses the hall for other activities such as PE lessons, CCA trainings and indoor games. Most students would remember the hall as a place where they first got their school badges pinned on them during their Year 1 Orientation
