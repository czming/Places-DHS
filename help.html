Using Places@DHS

Submitting your story!

To submit your story, you first have to log into your DHS account by clicking on the Log In button on the right side of the navigation bar.

After doing so, click on the Submit button in the navigation bar to see the Submit Your Story form

Select the place which you would like to share your story about and fill up the text box with your story then click the 'Submit Your Story' button to submit your story

Seeing other peoples' stories

On the home page, click on the image or the name of a place to see the place page which has a more detailed place description and the stories from other Dunmanians

On the page, you can see the place description or scroll below and see the stories shared by other Dunmanians


Have fun using Places@DHS!
